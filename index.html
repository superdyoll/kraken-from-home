This weeks challenges

Challenge

Run the Fleur-de-lis

Use your favourite run/cycling/walking tracking app to draw a fleur-de-lis.
Get outside.
Use your one exercise per day to challenge yourself to draw something. The better the fleur-de-lis the more points you'll get. You don't have to do it at any particular speed so you can choose if you want to run, walk or cycle!

Activity

April Fools

Play a kind April fools joke on one of your household. Don't be mean to them though. Just some lighthearted fun. The more the video or photo of your prank makes us laugh the more points you get!


How to submit

To submit your photos or videos you can either send them to our instagram @krakenesu, our facebook group krakenesu, our facebook page with a DM krakensu or you can email them to kraken@southamptoncityscouts.org.uk
